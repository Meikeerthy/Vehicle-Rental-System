Vehicles must be used responsibly and within legal speed limits. Off-road driving, racing, or using the vehicle for commercial purposes is strictly prohibited.

All rentals come with basic insurance coverage. Customers are liable for damages beyond normal wear and tear.

Vehicles must be returned with the same fuel level as at pickup. Additional charges may apply for refueling.

6. Cancellation & Refunds

7. Breakdown Assistance

24/7 roadside assistance is available for all rentals. In case of an emergency, contact our helpline.

8. Late Returns & Extensions

Late returns may be subject to additional charges. Extensions must be requested in advance.

Customer information is handled securely and will not be shared with third parties without consent.
